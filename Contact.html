About Us

"Beauty Princess Salon & Spa has been the culmination of years of attention to the finer detail. We use our own products & services and strive to only provide services that we'll use ourselves. Come on over and we'll make you feel rested and right at home."

We offer a wide range of hair services, including haircuts, color, highlights, and styling, as well as a variety of beauty treatments, such as facials, waxing, and nail services. Our team is dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques in hair and beauty, and we use only the highest-quality products to ensure that you look and feel your best.

At Beauty Princess Salon & Spa, we believe that everyone deserves to be pampered, and we are committed to providing our clients with the best possible experience. Whether you're looking for a quick touch-up or a full day of relaxation, we're here to help you look and feel your best.
